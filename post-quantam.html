Digital Experience Service

Engaging, Empowering, Educational

The Digital Experience can result in these types of outcomes and include many more based upon the transformation of data into meaningful information. The business can translate their organizational goals into information that can be shared with audiences across the digital device landscape. In order to enable these communication channels and create new Digital Experiences, a Creative Brief is required. Read more

The Creative Brief links the business goals to the content strategy that outlines the creative approach and delivery to market. The guidelines for personas, messaging, tools, content creation, and deliverables are included which result in content assets such as video, audio, or text. These assets are associated with marketing channels and their respective metrics and success criteria. It is an approach that most Marketing Departments, Agencies and Creative Studios utilize for launching campaigns. DUSA Digital Experience Service provides a consultative approach to conduct an assessment of your data, information, and content. The assessment includes a determination for Expression Capabilities for shaping the personal user experiences in Extended Realties. This also includes content delivered to Virtual, Augmented and Mixed Realities.

Quantum STEP Services

Security and encryption

The Quantum STEP Service enables organizations to determine the required security posture for a hybrid encryption solution that includes Post-Quantum Cryptography and Classical Encryption. The assessment includes a review of:

  • Business Events that trigger workflows and workload processing
  • Expressions associated with the underlying platform technologies
  • Enabling Experiences that map to user and group personas
  • Outcomes that are based upon metrics and success criteria
