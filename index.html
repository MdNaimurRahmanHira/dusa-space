Cryptography Service

The Cryptography Service utilizes a suite of tools that enables an organization to become Quantum-Safe. These tools include upgraded algorithms that comprise the Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) encryption methods. Quantum Random Number Generators (QRNG), Quantum Key Management Systems (QKS), Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) and Quantum Secure Layer (QSL) protocols constitute the Quantum Suite of Cryptography Services.

DUSA provides a consultative engagement to:
  • Assess the current encryption security strength employed throughout the organization
  • Readiness to adopt PQC in Proof-of-Concept lab trials
  • PQC Plan to conduct Penetration and Vulnerability Tests
